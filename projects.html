Collections of no-touch instruments

This page brings together a growing family of no-touch exploratory tools designed to spark curiosity, wonder, creativity, and wellbeing. Each tool invites children to engage with materials, sounds, movement, and sensory experiences through open-ended exploration. Rather than focusing on a predefined outcome, these experiences encourage observation, experimentation, self-expression, and self-regulation. To help navigate the collection, the tools are organized into seven categories that reflect the primary type of experience they offer:

  • Material Exploration
  • Discovery & Exploration
  • Wonder & Curiosity
  • Sound Exploration
  • Movement & Transformation
  • Creation & Composition
  • Wellbeing & Self-Regulation
While each tool belongs to a main category, many naturally connect to multiple areas (experience), creating rich opportunities for play, learning, and personal growth.

🌿 Material Exploration

Tools that invite to investigate textures, materials, surfaces, weight, and physical properties through open-ended exploration.

Tool Description Experience

Dust

 An exploratory tool that invites to observe, manipulate, and interact with fine particles, revealing patterns, movements, and transformations within a constantly changing material landscape.
  • Transformation
  • Sound exploration

Liquid Threshold

 Explores liquidity as a relational state between gesture, image, and sound. Through hand tracking, each crossing of a threshold generates luminous efflorescences and aquatic resonances, transforming movement into a form of sensitive matter.
  • Self-Regulation
  • Sound exploration

🌊 Sound Exploration

Listening, creating and understanding sound. Interactive experiences where sound becomes a tool for exploration, expression, and learning.

Tool Description Experience

Elastico

 Elastico is designed to explore the relationship between colors and frequency-modulation (FM) synthesis.
  • Sound exploration
  • Movement & Transformation
