NoTouch Instruments

Inclusive music technology and gesture‑based musical interaction

No‑touch instruments are digital musical interfaces that allow users to play, shape and explore sound without any physical contact. They rely on hand‑tracking, computer vision and gesture‑based interaction to translate movement into expressive musical actions.

These instruments are designed to be accessible, intuitive and inclusive, enabling meaningful musical experiences for everyone — including individuals with motor disabilities, special educational needs and diverse learning profiles.
